NOUFLEX is a new versatile and compact home-gym exercise equipment designed to more effectively train your body. It is capable of performing well over 250 exercises. It's only 5' x 2'.5" feet; weighs less than 40lbs, 10 levels of resistance and very portable. Shipping is FREE and delivery will be made during the first week of March 2015. The first 100 units of NOUFLEX come with a workout manual &
three free home-visit workouts.
